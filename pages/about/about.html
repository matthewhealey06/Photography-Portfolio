I'm a self taught Photographer
based in the Cheshire region
I love taking photos of a variety of things
mostly which includes wildlife.

However I also experiment in
Portraits, Travel, Pets, Automotive
and any little extra projects I might find myself interested in.

Fun fact: I actually started on the other side of the camera
Growing up I wanted to be an actor, however in 2024
I lost my love for acting. Which was then rediscovered in 2025.
This time behind the camera.

Initially I started watching a YouTuber called Simon D'entrement
I love the little facts and help he would offer
And watched many of his videos, before even having a camera!

And now that I think, thats probably one of the reasons I'm so interested in wildlife.
That and the endless possibilities.
Well... the way I look at it is;

You can stage a portrait
You can stage automotive photography
But wildlife? Thats something you cant stage.
You cant tell a lion to start yawning

And thats what I love
The total unpredictability of what can happen
The times when youre just 1 second away from getting your hero shot.
And the times where you're just 1 second too late.
