Tatton Park

I SEE YOU

When I went to West Midlands Safari Park the cheetah bit was new.

As I started taking pictures I noticed its eyes would follow me wherever I would go, no matter how many other people were around. So either it thought I was its next meal or it just loved posing for the camera. Either way, I loved taking photos of the cheetah and moving to different places to see if it would continue to look at me which It did.

Then as I was walking away and was already quite far away, I turned around to see if it was still looking at me through my lens and to my surprise it still was!
