THE DUNES

Taken in Ica, Peru. I just got there in time to see the sunset. Which I quickly took out my camera to take this photo knowing I would cherish the dynamic range capable within my camera.

Fun Fact about this photo is I nearly couldnt take it at all, it turns out in order to get onto the dunes, you needed cash to buy a ticket, however since I had no cash I was stood at the entrance not knowing what to do. Which kindly the guy at the enterance said that I could go through for free (probably knowing that sunset was nearly over)
